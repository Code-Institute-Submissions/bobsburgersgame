Rules.

Flip the burger over to reveal the front face.

Match two of the front facing burgers you have flipped.

You can only flip two burgers a time. If they match they stay facing up. If they dont match they flip back!

To complete the game you need to match all burgers with their front face.

Your time starts, on the first flip and you are counted on the number of burger combinations it takes to complete the game.
